Our Services

 

We provide door to door, round trip service. It’s necessary to give our clients a secure feeling of not having to inconvenience them or their friends and family. We also provide one-way services for people who need to get home or be driven to their doctor appointments or medical procedures. With how busy our schedule is we do require a $100 deposit. MCS follows the laws of the privacy of our clients and will provide our clients with a HIPPA law document explaining their rights to privacy while being escorted to medical appointments.
